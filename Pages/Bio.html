My Bio

My name is JD Holwick, and I am from Missoula, MT where I am also finishing my degree. Currently, I am completing my final six credits to graduate with my B.S. in computer science. During the summer of 2022 I interned at Edulog and currently work there part-time moving into a salaried position immediately after graduation. A little history about Edulog. -- Edulog, or the fullname, Educational Logistics, Inc., is a school bus routing software company. They have been operating since 1977 and started in my hometown of Missoula, MT. They provide services to schools all over the Untied States and Canada.
